Terms & Conditions

Payment

50% upfront is required before I start. Final payment before delivery. PayPal only. No refunds after sketch approval.

Usage

Commissioned work is for personal use only. Commercial use requires separate agreement and pricing. You may post the art with proper credit.

Revisions

2 minor revisions included. Major changes or full redesigns after sketch approval will incur additional fees.

Timeframe

Delivery time varies (1–4 weeks). I'll keep you updated. Rush orders available at +50%.

Rights

I retain the right to post finished commissions in my portfolio unless you request otherwise (confidentiality fee applies).

Cancellations

If you cancel after the sketch is complete, the upfront fee is non-refundable. I reserve the right to refuse any commission.

FAQ

Are your commissions open? +
Yes! Commissions are currently open. DM me on Instagram or send me an email with your request and I'll get back to you as soon as possible.
Are you open to collaborations? +
Absolutely! I love collaborating on creative projects — games, zines, websites, or any cross-discipline work. Reach out and let's talk about it.
Can I repost your work? +
You may repost with clear credit back to me. Please do not crop out watermarks, claim the work as your own, or use it for AI training datasets.
Which softwares do you use? +
For digital art I use MedBang Pro. For game dev I use Ren'Py and Unity. For design work I use Figma. For coding I mostly use VSCode.
What's your setup? +
I draw on a drawing tablet connected to my laptop. Nothing fancy — just passion and a lot of late night sessions.
