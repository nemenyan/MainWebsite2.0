FAQ

Are your commissions open? +
For commissions work, DM me on Instagram or Discord @nemenyan with your request and I'll get back to you as soon as possible.
Are you open to collaborations? +
Absolutely! I enjoy working with other creatives on projects, whether it's game development, digital art, or web experiences. Feel free to reach out via email or social media for further discussions.
Can I repost your work? +
  • Please ask my permission first via dm/email!
  • If you do repost my work, please credit me and link back to my profile!
    • Though as a general rule, please don't repost my work in pages where they're already posted by me, I already have socials for these, please feel free to share them instead!
    Which softwares do you use? +
    For digital art I use MedBang Pro. For game dev I use Ren'Py and slowly learning Unity. For design work I use Figma. For coding I mostly use VSCode.
    What's your setup? +
    I use a HUION KAMVAS Pro 22 GT-221 Monitor to draw.
    My PC specifications are the following:
    • CPU: 13th Gen Intel® Core™ i7-13700F
    • GPU: NVIDIA GeForce RTX 4070
    • RAM: 32GB DDR5
